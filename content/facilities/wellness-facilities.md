### Yoga Shala

## Section 4 — Steam Room (stacked content section)
### Steam Room

Our steam room is a specially designed room using a humid heat source. The heat source usually comes from a steam generator. Steam rooms enable healing and rejuvenation to the body with heated temperatures. Steam rooms have specific health benefits such as clearing congestion, improving skin health, lowering blood pressure improving circulation, workout recovery, relaxation, and reducing joint stiffness.

## Section 7 — Contras Pool (stacked content section with images)
### Contras Pool

The Contrast Pool experience combines a Cold Plunge (5–10°C) and Warm Plunge (40°C) for powerful therapeutic benefits. Alternating between cold and warm immersion enhances circulation, reduces inflammation, eases muscle tension, and supports recovery. This invigorating ritual boosts energy, improves sleep, and promotes holistic wellness for body and mind.
