## Section 1 — Main Content (single column with heading and subsections)
### Gift Shop

There's always something for everyone to bring home, and that is all our gift shop is about. Our range of gifts has been carefully curated to represent the Gdas Bali experience wrapped in our bespoke packaging. Whether clothing, souvenirs, or beauty products, every item reflects the charm, elegance, luxe of our resort to help recall the thoughtful and welcoming service we offer.

### health & wellness.

We have sourced only the best brands to offer you a handpicked selection of health and wellness products to supplement your experience with us, and they are also great as gifts to take home.

### sustainable products.

We have sourced the market for you and have a wide selection of sustainable products. Everything from locally made bamboo straws, water bottles, tote bags for your daily activities and so much more.
