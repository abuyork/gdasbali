## Section 1 — Main Content (single column)
### The Library

There's more than enough to keep you entertained in Gdas Bali; visiting our library may be the ideal way to win down at the end of a long day of sightseeing. Our unique reading room with floor-to-ceiling bookshelves features numerous books collections about Usadha Bali and Balinese cultures that will pique your interest. It is also equipped with advanced technology and entertainment as a place for you to relax. Find your way to our library and explore our books collections.

- Opening hours: 08:00 – 20:00
