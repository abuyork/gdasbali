### Swimming Pool

Take a relaxing dip into paradise. Soak up the Bali sunshine by submerging yourself in our swimming pool overlooking the lush rice paddies. Or hideaway in a luxe-shaded cabana available for you to use for the day. Enjoy our mouth-watering vegan menus and wash them down with our signature drinks that will leave your taste buds tingling for more. Nothing can truly compare to this feeling.

- Cabana information: Open for in-house guests only
- Opening hours: 09:00 – 20:00
