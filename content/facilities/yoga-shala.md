## Section 1 — Main Content (single column)
### Yoga Shala

Gdas Bali's yoga shala is a five-star yoga space with high-quality classes taught by world-class professional yoga teachers. Our yoga shala is well equipped and made of Ubud's tranquil beauty with views of rice fields and jungle. It is a perfect place to find daily yoga classes, yoga teacher training, and holistic wellness treatments under one roof. It is a true haven for yogis, and to top it all off, the whole property is smoke and alcohol-free.

Our yoga shala is also open as a wedding chapel, a romantic place for you to say 'I do.' Our concierge service will take care of your event, from the wedding dress, photography, flowers, and reception to all kinds of entertainment. You will be enjoying your special day with ease to help make your day flawless. For further inquiries, please contact us at sales@gdasbali.com.

- Opening hours: 07:00 – 17:00
