### Pranayama Yoga*
Pranayama is the yogic science of using the breath for the health of the body and mind. You can control the subtle prana inside by exercising control over the gross breath. The aim of pranayama is the control of prana. If you can control prana, you can completely control all the forces of the universe, mental and physical.

Most people need to remember how to breathe properly. It is astonishing how little attention we pay to breathe correctly in everyday life. The breath is the most powerful manifestation of life. Life is breath. When the breath ceases, life ceases. The most important point to understand is the relationship between breath and prana. This science of using the breath to control the prana (or vital energy) is called pranayama.
