### SLEEP WELL

Do you feel refreshed upon waking up in the morning? Our sleep quality is often overlooked. The intensity of work or stress forces us to stay awake and pass the ideal sleeping hours. A night of good sleep is one of the most important aspects required for maintaining and achieving a healthier body, mind, and soul, especially for helping prevent premature aging. When your body gets the sleep it needs, your immune cells and proteins get the rest they need to fight off whatever comes their way. Our specially designed deep relaxation program will help you enhance your sleep quality.

### Trataka Meditation
Trataka, a technique used in meditation, is one of the six purification techniques, called shatkarmas, of Hatha yoga. Trataka is a Sanskrit word, which means "to look" or "to gaze." As such, this meditation technique starts at a single focus point. This is typically the flame of a candle, but other objects that may be used include a dot on the wall, an object of worship, a deity, a flower, a mountain, rising sun, or a moon. However, a flame is believed to work best. Meditating in this way is believed to energize the ajna (third eye) Chakra, which is associated with intuition, wisdom, and psychic abilities. Traditionally, it's said that the practice allows the past, present and future to be perceived with equal clarity.
