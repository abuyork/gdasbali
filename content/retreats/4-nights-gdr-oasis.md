## Section 6 — Retreat Leaders (Three profile cards with photos and bios)
### Retreat Leaders

#### Gaile
Gaile, of mixed Chinese and Vietnamese descent, grew up in the United States before moving to Hong Kong at 18 to pursue a modeling career. Amidst her success in the fashion world, she discovered yoga at 23, a challenge that sparked a competitive drive and a passion for personal growth. Her journey in yoga led her to acquire 200 hours of training in Vinyasa and 100 hours in Rocket Ashtanga, blending the traditional and modern dynamics of the practice.

Yoga transformed Gaile, teaching her to reflect and grow, fostering a sense of grounding and mindfulness that extended beyond the mat. Her philosophy centers on self-love, respect, and gratitude, emphasizing personal growth and community connection. Gaile champions a lifestyle of presence and kindness, advocating for continuous learning and self-nurturing.

Gaile has recently shifted her focus from modeling to the grounding forces of yoga and holistic wellness. Her life now harmonizes the elegance of the fashion world with the earthiness of farming and nature, embodying a journey of balanced and mindful living.

This practice not only became her sanctuary amidst life's storms, but it also illuminated a path of purpose. Feeling the profound shift, Doris felt a calling to intertwine her learnings of yoga into her everyday life, both on the mat and off the mat. ## Section 7 — What to Expect While in Bali (Multi-subsection with image galleries)

### Gdas Bali
Nestled amidst the beautiful Balinese countryside, Gdas Bali is a luxurious retreat offering 27 stunning rooms and villas. It is the perfect destination for the discerning traveller who is seeking a premier resort experience within a stunning and exclusive environment, paired with excellent accommodation and genuine Balinese hospitality from the heart. Guests will wake up to the soothing sounds of nature, in a cocoon of rest and relaxation. Guests can choose from 2 accommodation categories, both beautifully and thoughtfully designed and to embrace the natural surroundings and maximize natural sunlight. Facilities and amenities include a spa and wellness centre, a fitness centre, a library, an event space, a vegan restaurant, a resort boutique and two yoga studios that overlook the lush paddy fields which surround the resort. The resort is a seamless coming together of traditional Balinese architecture with modern and contemporary designs. Interiors are modern and sleek, paired with gorgeous intricate designs to highlight the culture and heritage of Bali. The work of Bali's talented craftsmen and artisans can clearly be seen here in selected ornaments and artworks, created lovingly from scratch by the artists of Mas village. The entire room emanates a feeling of warmth, comfort, and ease – a home away from home where you can fully relax.
