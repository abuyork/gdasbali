### Gdas Bali
Nestled amidst the beautiful Balinese countryside, Gdas Bali is a luxurious retreat offering 27 stunning rooms and villas. It is the perfect destination for the discerning traveller who is seeking a premier resort experience within a stunning and exclusive environment, paired with excellent accommodation and genuine Balinese hospitality from the heart. Guests will wake up to the soothing sounds of nature, in a cocoon of rest and relaxation. Guests can choose from 2 accommodation categories, both beautifully and thoughtfully designed and to embrace the natural surroundings and maximize natural sunlight. Facilities and amenities include a spa and wellness centre, a fitness centre, a library, an event space, a vegan restaurant, a resort boutique and two yoga studios that overlook the lush paddy fields which surround the resort. The resort is a seamless coming together of traditional Balinese architecture with modern and contemporary designs. Interiors are modern and sleek, paired with gorgeous intricate designs to highlight the culture and heritage of Bali. The work of Bali's talented craftsmen and artisans can clearly be seen here in selected ornaments and artworks, created lovingly from scratch by the artists of Mas village. The entire room emanates a feeling of warmth, comfort, and ease – a home away from home where you can fully relax.
