### Description
Becoming a yogipreneur is an exhilarating journey, but it also comes with its unique set of challenges.

This retreat is for you if you're a new teacher who dreams of…
- Launching your yoga business and becoming a yogipreneur, embracing the freedom and fulfillment of leading a heart-centered life.
- Transitioning from your corporate career to a heart-led business, allowing passion and purpose to guide your journey.
- Deepening your yoga practice and teaching, immersing in the transformative power of yoga to grow both personally and professionally.
- Creating sequences that not only feel good in the body but also nurture emotional and mental well-being, crafting a holistic experience for your students.
- Engaging in self-discovery practices to remove limiting beliefs and blocks, unlocking your potential and greatness as a yoga teacher.
- Theming impactful classes using philosophy and storytelling, weaving wisdom and inspiration into every session.
- Finding your unique voice and niche as a yoga teacher, standing out and making a meaningful impact in the world

Or a current teacher who feels stagnant and uninspired due to…
- Repetition from teaching the same classes leading to lack of enthusiasm and creativity.
- Burnout from the physical and emotional demands of teaching and stress of running a business.
- Feelings of isolation especially for those who teach independently.

This is a time for new or current teachers to embrace their vulnerabilities, seek support, and remain committed to their passion for sharing the transformative power of yoga.

Many new yoga teachers find themselves navigating the uncertainty of building a student base, creating engaging class sequences, and standing out in a saturated market. The transition to becoming teacher can feel overwhelming, as you strive to deepen your own practice while also finding your unique voice and teaching style. Balancing self-doubt with the desire to make a meaningful impact can be daunting, but it's also a period of immense growth and discovery.

This is for the yoga teacher who wants to understand:
- how to show up as a leader in the wellness space

- how to explore diverse income streams from your yoga and wellness business
- how to manage your finances with ease (yoga biz finance 101)
- how to master the essentials of email marketing and creating sales funnels
- how to build and nurture a thriving community

Join me on an empowering journey on this enchanting island that I now call home.

14 years ago I visited Bali for the first time and I just knew. I felt such a strong connection to Mama Bali. People always ask me what is it that I love about Bali and if I'm being honest I can't really explain it. But here, I feel loved, I feel supported, I feel free and I feel magical.⁠⁠

I'm overflowed with gratitude for being here and I can't wait to share this experience with you. Bali's healing energy and sacred traditions provide the perfect backdrop for your transformative journey.

### Gdas Bali
Nestled amidst the beautiful Balinese countryside, Gdas Bali is a luxurious retreat offering 27 stunning rooms and villas. It is the perfect destination for the discerning traveller who is seeking a premier resort experience within a stunning and exclusive  environment, paired with excellent accommodation and genuine Balinese hospitality from the heart. Guests will wake up to the soothing sounds of nature, in a cocoon of rest and relaxation. Guests can choose from 2 accommodation categories, both beautifully and thoughtfully designed and to embrace the natural surroundings and maximize natural sunlight. Facilities and amenities include a spa and wellness centre, a fitness centre, a library, an event space, a vegan restaurant, a resort boutique and two yoga studios that overlook the lush paddy fields which surround the resort.  The resort is a seamless coming together of traditional Balinese architecture with modern and contemporary designs. Interiors are modern and sleek, paired with gorgeous intricate designs to highlight the culture and heritage of Bali. The work of Bali's talented craftsmen and artisans can clearly be seen here in selected ornaments and artworks, created lovingly from scratch by the artists of Mas village. The entire room emanates a feeling of warmth, comfort, and ease – a home away from home where you can fully relax.
