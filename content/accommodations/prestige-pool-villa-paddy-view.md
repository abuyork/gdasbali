### Prestige Pool Villa
#### Prestige Pool Villa Paddy View

An exceptional 2-storey abode nestled within the lush surroundings of Kumbuh, our Prestige Pool Villa features a uniquely hybrid design that bridges the modern with the traditional.

A striking glass-wood-stone structure, each villa feature elements inspired by traditional Indonesian homes from across the archipelago, a nod to the regal Javanese joglo with its uniquely distinct roofs and the Malay traditional houses of Sumatra with their tall stilts and imposing pillars.

On the ground floor, guests can look forward to living their island dreams in the semi-open living and dining areas that overlook the pool and the clear cool water. There is also a fully-equipped kitchenette and floor-to-ceiling glass doors and windows.

Upstairs, premium super king-sized beds nestle amidst lavish surroundings. Under beautifully designed vaulted ceilings, guests can unwind in the sumptuous ambience, with almost 360-degree views of the rice paddies and jungles beyond.

All three of the Prestige Pool Villas were named after Balinese Hindu philosophy and ethical teachings; Tri Kaya Parisudha. The word Tri means Three, Kaya means attitude, and Parisudha means noble or pure. The three principles include Manacika which means good thinking, Wacika means good words and Kayika, which means doing good.
