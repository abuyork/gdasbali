## Section 2 — Collecting Identifiable Information
Gdas Bali Health and Wellness Resort receives and collects information:

- Entered on our websites and/or provided in various ways:
  - **Personally Identifiable Information**: May include but is not limited to guests' names, genders, dates of birth, home addresses, work addresses, email addresses, telephone numbers, fax numbers, occupations, IP addresses, browsers types, operating systems, visited pages, credit card information, membership details, and also joint travelers' details.
  - **Non-Personally Identifiable Information**: Including but not limited to guests' preferred communication methods, details related to guests' stays (arrival/departure dates, room preferences, flight details, etc.), as well as details related to responses to reservation confirmations, promotional offers, competitions, or surveys.
- Provided by other sources: We might also acquire information about guests from other sources and add it to guests' existing details.
- Information about visitors/guests is collected when you:
  - Visit our official websites and access the subpages (with or without registration)
  - Make offline reservations via email
  - Make online reservations via our booking engine (through the website of our service-provider partner)
  - Enter our competitions/contests/sweepstakes (with or without registration)
  - Submit inquiries, feedback, or comments using the 'Contact Us' form or similar feature on this website
  - Use online services on the website (buying gift vouchers, etc.)
  - Sign up for our updates/newsletters
  - Apply for jobs at Gdas Bali Health and Wellness Resort

## Section 3 — Cookies and Trackers
This website may use cookies, web beacons, invisible pixels, or other online technologies to enhance and simplify your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files sent to your browser from a web server, to then be stored on your hard disk. They remember your username/password for a faster login process and your customized preferences. They ensure that you obtain the appropriate content and all the requested information. Cookies enable our website to recognize repeat visitors, usage behavior, and also to compile provided data for content improvements, targeted advertising, and to better facilitate you in finding information on our website. Most browsers are, by default, set to accept cookies, but you can delete cookies from your browser at any time. Should you prefer to handle cookies acceptance case by case or not to accept cookies at all, there is a simple adjustment you will need to perform from your browser's settings. However, rejecting cookies might result in the blocking of certain features or transaction performance. Gdas Bali Health and Wellness Resort also uses web bugs/web beacons/invisible pixels to improve website contents and marketing activities. These will track non-personal identifiable information such as your IP address, your browser type, the time you requested our website content, which URL you visited our website from, and which pages (on our website) you have visited.

## Section 4 — Purposes of Collecting Information
Gdas Bali Health and Wellness Resort uses the collected information about you for the following primary purposes:

- **Extension of our Services**: To answer inquiries, fulfill requests, process reservations, finalize transactions, and customize our service to meet your preferences.
- **Marketing and Communications**: To keep you updated with our news, promotions, and competitions, and also for analytical and statistical purposes to measure your interests and improve our website as well as our services.
- **Job Applications**: To process job applications should you apply for an employment opportunity with us.

## Section 5 — The Security of Your Transaction and Information
To protect your information, online transactions via our booking engine use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology, which encrypts all information and is certified by a Secure Server Certification Authority.

## Section 6 — How We Share Your Information
Gdas Bali Health and Wellness Resort handles the collected information wisely and in general practices use them solely for on-site services (your stay arrangements, spa treatments, private dining, or other requested personalized arrangements) and for our own database. In certain cases when necessary and required, with or without your permission, we may disclose your information to other third parties such as:

- **Service Providers**: Our booking/reservation engine partner (gdasbali.com) or other services that you have requested (e.g., outsourced outdoor activities like golf, rafting, parks, day-trip tours, etc.), flight-ticket booking, or private-car rentals.
- **Business Transactions**: In the event of a business development, merger, or restructuring, we might—in our sole discretion—transfer or sell information collected which includes personal and non-personal information to one or more affiliated or unaffiliated third parties.
- **Others**: To comply with legal or regulatory obligations according to the applicable law or court order and to respond to an emergency such as to protect the safety of our property, guests, employees, or the public.

In the case that information is shared due to the above reasons, the use of the information will be kept limited to the necessity for the performance of merely the specific function.
